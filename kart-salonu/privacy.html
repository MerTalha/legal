Privacy Policy

Kart Salonu · MBD Dev · Last updated: June 27, 2025

Short version: Kart Salonu is a card game that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect personal data, upload game data to any server, or share your information with third parties — except as described below for ads.

1. Who We Are

Kart Salonu is developed and maintained by MBD Dev. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at mbddev2@gmail.com.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your game history, scores, or in-app progress (stored locally on your device only)
  • Your location
  • Your contacts, camera, or microphone data

All game logic and statistics run entirely on your device and are never sent to any server.

3. Data Collected by Third Parties

3.1 Advertising (Google AdMob)

Kart Salonu displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used solely to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads). See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Permissions

  • Internet: Required to load advertisements.
  • Vibration: Used for optional haptic feedback during gameplay. No data is collected.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on servers. Game statistics and settings are saved locally on your device and remain under your full control. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Kart Salonu is a card game appropriate for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The ads served comply with Google AdMob's family policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at mbddev2@gmail.com.
